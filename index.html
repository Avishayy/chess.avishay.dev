Chess Position Explorer

An interactive chessboard inside ChatGPT.

ChatGPT app · free

What it does

Chess Position Explorer adds a draggable chessboard to ChatGPT. Ask ChatGPT to set up a position — by name, by FEN, or from the starting position — and the board appears inline in the conversation. Drag pieces freely to explore variations, flip the board, or expand to fullscreen. ChatGPT sees your current position and can answer questions about it.

How to use it

  • Install the app in ChatGPT and start a conversation.
  • Ask something like open a chessboard, show me the Najdorf, or paste a FEN string.
  • Drag pieces to edit the position. Ask follow-up questions — ChatGPT reads the current board.
  • Click Expand for a larger view with a FEN readout and reset button.

Demo

What's under the hood

The app is a small MCP server that validates chess positions and returns a React widget. The board is rendered with chessground (Lichess's board component) and positions are validated with chess.js. The piece sprites and knight silhouette in the app icon are by Colin M.L. Burnett, licensed under CC BY-SA 3.0.
